# LabelHead Go-To-Market

## Four Active Tracks

**Track 1 — The Capitalist (VC Outreach)**
Goal: Raise capital from music-tech and cultural investment funds.
Priority: 3rd — runs in parallel, reinforces sponsor pitch
Key activity: Personalized VC outreach, deck sharing, follow-up cadence

**Track 2 — The Dealmaker (Sponsor Outreach)**
Goal: Close at least one brand sponsor deal before Founding Season launch.
Priority: 1st — this is the highest-priority track. No sponsor = no prize pool.
Key activity: Outbound to brands with cultural cachet in music and culture

**Track 3 — The Publisher (Content + Waitlist)**
Goal: Build waitlist demand exceeding supply before invites open.
Priority: 2nd — demand must exceed supply before launch
Key activity: X content strategy, waitlist growth, community building

**Track 4 — The Curator (Community Seeding)**
Goal: Recruit founding competitors and seed credibility core.
Priority: 4th — credibility core before scale
Key activity: Direct outreach to A&R professionals, tastemakers, music journalists

## Priority Hierarchy

1. Sponsor pipeline — no sponsor = no prize pool
2. Content and waitlist — demand must exceed supply
3. VC outreach — parallel to sponsors, reinforces the pitch
4. Community seeding — credibility before scale
