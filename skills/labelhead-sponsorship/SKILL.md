# Sponsor Outreach Strategy

## Why Sponsors Are Priority One

LabelHead cannot launch Founding Season without a prize pool. The prize pool requires a sponsoring brand. This is not a nice-to-have — it is the critical path dependency for the entire launch.

## Target Sponsor Profile

**Ideal sponsor characteristics:**
- Brand with authentic cultural presence in music (not brands that force their way into music)
- Willing to be associated with talent discovery and cultural foresight — not just chart success
- Marketing budget that aligns with a targeted, high-credibility audience ($50K–$500K deal range)
- Decision-maker who understands cultural positioning — not just impressions

**Priority categories:**
- Streaming platforms and music tech (DSPs, DAW companies, pro audio)
- Lifestyle brands with genuine music culture presence (streetwear, footwear)
- Financial services targeting creatives and artists
- Label services companies (distribution, publishing administration)

**Do not pursue:**
- Brands that have no authentic music connection (forces alignment)
- Brands that have previously embarrassed themselves in music partnerships
- Any brand requiring exclusive category rights that would limit future partnerships

## Outreach Framework

**Opening premise**: LabelHead is building the first structured competition for cultural foresight in music. The first season's winner isn't the person who picked the most popular artist — it's the person who saw it earliest. That story is worth telling alongside a brand.

**What a sponsorship provides:**
- Association with cultural discovery, not just popularity
- Access to a high-quality audience of A&R professionals and music insiders
- A narrative angle: brand helped crown the first proven cultural forecaster in music

**What it does not provide:**
- Mass reach (LabelHead's audience is small and highly targeted)
- General consumer brand awareness
- Social media followers

## Sponsor Pipeline Tracker

The Sponsor Pipeline is tracked in the "VC Pipeline" Google Sheet (shared with ATLAS). The Dealmaker maintains this tracker. When The Acquisitor identifies newsletter sponsorship opportunities, they are appended to the tracker with source: "The Acquisitor".

## Outreach Governance

- All sponsor commitments require Geoffrey's explicit approval before confirmation
- Deal terms are draft-only until Geoffrey reviews
- The Dealmaker can negotiate but cannot commit
- Report all responses to ATLAS — do not wait for the next heartbeat to escalate a live response
