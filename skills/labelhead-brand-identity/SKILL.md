# LabelHead Brand Identity

## Voice

LabelHead's voice is Cabinet Grotesk: restrained, data-grounded, authoritative. The brand earns attention by being specific and precise, not by shouting.

**Rules:**
- No exclamation points in professional content
- No hype language ("viral", "buzzing", "blowing up", "on fire")
- No emoji in professional or founder content
- No unconfirmed partnerships — reference only confirmed facts
- No prize pool figures until officially announced
- Avoid superlatives unless they can be backed by data
- Write for the skeptic, not the fan

## Terminology

Always use:
- "Cycle" — not "week" or "round"
- "Competition" — not "game" or "challenge"
- "Cultural foresight" — not "trend-spotting" or "vibes"
- "Founding Competitors" — the early cohort
- "Founding Season" — the launch period
- "LabelHead" — always one word, capital L and H

Never use:
- "Week" (as a LabelHead time unit)
- "Viral" as a descriptor for artists
- References to Timbaland or unannounced partnerships
- Specific prize pool amounts

## Copy Standards

**Headlines**: Specific declarative statements or direct questions. No listicle energy ("5 reasons..."). No clickbait qualifiers ("you won't believe...").

**Opening lines**: Answer the headline in the first two sentences. No preamble.

**Social copy**: Short. Specific. The strongest point leads. No thread intros ("🧵 A thread on..."). No trail-off endings ("Just a thought...").

**CTAs**: Understated. "Compete at labelhead.co" is sufficient. Never "Sign up NOW" or any urgency-based pressure.

## Audience

Primary: A&R professionals, music journalists, tastemakers, music executives.
Secondary: VCs and investors interested in music tech and cultural data.

Write for people who are genuinely knowledgeable about music culture. Never explain concepts they already know. Never condescend to the fan audience — speak to the industry insider.

## Channels

**X (@labelheadco)**: Primary distribution. Thread format for substantive takes. Single tweets for cultural commentary. No reposting noise.

**Email**: Eventual channel. Currently building list. Direct subject lines, short body, one CTA.

**Website (labelhead.co)**: Authoritative, product-forward. The hub that all other content references.
