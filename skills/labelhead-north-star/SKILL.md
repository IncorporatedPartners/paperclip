# LabelHead North Star

## The Vision

LabelHead becomes the infrastructure for cultural foresight in music. The long-term aspiration is a Bloomberg Terminal for music taste — a data system that makes taste measurable, A&R decisions auditable, and cultural judgment a trackable record.

The near-term product (the competition) is the acquisition mechanism. The long-term product is the data system.

## The Thesis

Cultural foresight in music is currently invisible infrastructure. A&R professionals, music executives, and tastemakers make consequential judgment calls that are never tracked, never scored, never compared. LabelHead changes that.

The competition creates the data: who signed which artist, when, at what signal level. Over cycles, this builds a public record of taste — who was early, who was late, who saw what others missed.

## The Scoring System

LabelHead scores cultural foresight across four dimensions:

- **Acceleration**: Rate of growth vs. baseline expectations
- **Surprise**: How much the artist outperformed prediction
- **Longevity**: Sustained momentum over time
- **Cultural Gravity**: How much culture bends toward the artist — collaborations, references, influence

These dimensions combine into a single momentum signal per artist. The signal is honest — it reflects genuine assessment, not marketing positioning.

## Feature Evaluation Criteria

When evaluating features or strategic decisions, ask:

1. Does this build the data layer, or just the game layer?
2. Does this attract people who are right before others, or people who follow trends?
3. Would an A&R professional use this? Would a music journalist trust it?
4. Does this compound over time (data, reputation, network) or does it reset each cycle?

## What LabelHead Is Not

- Not a ranking or chart service
- Not a streaming analytics dashboard
- Not a social prediction market (no money wagering)
- Not a fan competition (audiences pick favorites)
- Not A&R itself — LabelHead surfaces signals, doesn't sign artists

## Founding Season Context

The Founding Season is the launch cohort — a limited group of early competitors who get lifetime enhanced status and pricing. Demand must exceed supply before invites open. The goal is to launch oversubscribed.
